About Me

My History

A breif history of how I got where I am today.

Childhood
Originaly I got introduced to programming as an extra curicular class in elementary school invovling a "block-placed logic" application to make simple games and programs.
Adolescence
I took classes in middle-school involving "Blitz3D" and its predecessor "Blitz Basic" to make video-games, such as, space invadors and pacman.
In high-school I branched out to more languages such as Java, JavaScript, HTML and CSS, there were no classes available for programming beyond what they offered in middle school.
Adulthood
Even though my family didn't have the money to send me to college I went on federal assistance.
I studied a semester of Computer Science, taking Web Design learing HTML, CSS and JavaScript as well as Computer Science LVL 1, using Python and Java.
I was forced to drop out due to finacial reasons then juggled jobs until finally joining the Air Force in 2018.
