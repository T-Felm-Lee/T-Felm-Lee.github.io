About PiF

This page is in work but if you've happened to stumble upon it and want to know more please feel free to send us an email to find out more. As this site grows and so does our mission to pay it forward we appreciate any feedback you want to provide. Our goal is to start a program that can not only encourage others to put their best foot forward and help someone in need, whether its holding the door open for someone or paying for the next persons drink in line. We want to create a community that can both support and encourage each other in new ways each and every day. Thank you for showing an interest and doing your part in contributing to a better tomorrow.
